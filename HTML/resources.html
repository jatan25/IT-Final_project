Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll by visiting the Home page and clicking the 'Enroll Now' button or directly contacting us through the Contact page.

Are the courses free?

Some courses are free, while others may require a fee. Check the course details for more information.

How can I contact my instructor?

You can contact your instructor through the 'Instructors' page, where you will find their email and contact details.

Can I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, upon successful completion of a course, you will receive a certificate that you can share with potential employers.

What should I do if I need help with a course?

If you need help, visit the 'Support' section in your course dashboard or contact the instructor directly through the 'Instructors' page.
